Solutions

Actions communities and individuals can take to reduce climate impacts and increase resilience.

  • Plant Trees & Restore
    Restore mangroves and forests to stabilize coasts and absorb carbon. Community planting programs with native species give long-term benefits.
  • Reduce, Reuse, Recycle
    Minimize waste, set up collection points and encourage reuse. Proper waste management reduces blockages that cause flooding.
  • Adopt Renewable Energy
    Solar and small-scale renewables reduce reliance on diesel and kerosene, lowering emissions and improving local energy access.
  • Improve Drainage & Waste Management
    Maintain drains, clear blockages and design green infrastructure to reduce flood impact — community-led maintenance is effective.

Getting started

Start with small, locally led projects: community clean-ups, tree nurseries, and mapping vulnerable areas. Partner with local authorities and NGOs for training and funding.
