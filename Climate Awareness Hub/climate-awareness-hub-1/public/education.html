Education on Climate Change

What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to significant changes in global temperatures and weather patterns over time. While climate change is a natural phenomenon, scientific evidence shows that human activities are currently driving an unprecedented rate of change.

Causes of Climate Change

  • Burning fossil fuels
  • Deforestation
  • Industrial processes
  • Agricultural practices

Effects of Climate Change

Climate change has far-reaching effects, including rising sea levels, extreme weather events, and loss of biodiversity.

Climate Challenges in Sierra Leone

Sierra Leone faces significant challenges due to climate change, including increased flooding, food insecurity, and health risks.

Flood Awareness Tips

  • Stay informed about weather forecasts.
  • Have an emergency plan in place.
  • Know your evacuation routes.

Environmental Protection Education

Understanding the importance of protecting our environment is crucial. Simple actions like reducing waste, conserving water, and using renewable energy can make a difference.
